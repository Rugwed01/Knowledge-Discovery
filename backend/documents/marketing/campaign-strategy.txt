Q4 Marketing Campaign Strategy

Objective:
Increase brand awareness by 25% and generate 500 new leads through targeted campaigns.

Target Audience:
- Age: 25-45
- Interests: Technology, Innovation
- Geographic: North America, Europe

Channels:
1. Social Media (LinkedIn, Twitter)
2. Email Marketing
3. Content Marketing
4. Paid Advertising

Budget Allocation:
- Social Media: 40%
- Email Marketing: 20%
- Content Marketing: 25%
- Paid Advertising: 15%

Key Metrics:
- Impressions
- Click-through Rate
- Conversion Rate
- Cost Per Lead
